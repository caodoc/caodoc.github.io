## OS: Windows 10

## Browser: Google Chrome (I tried Dev & Canary but it was buggy).

## Editor: Visual Studio Code, with:
* [Catppuccin Icons](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=Catppuccin.catppuccin-vsc-icons) (Mocha)
* [Catppuccin Theme](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=Catppuccin.catppuccin-vsc) (Frappé)

## Tools:
* [PowerToys](https://github.com/microsoft/PowerToys): Best app with utilities for Windows.
* [PowerTunnel](https://github.com/krlvm/PowerTunnel): An extensible proxy server.
* [HakuNeko](https://github.com/manga-download/hakuneko): Anime/Manga downloader.
* [Notepad++](https://notepad-plus-plus.org): Quick files open without waiting for VSCode.
* [YTDownloader](https://github.com/aandrew-me/ytDownloader): Youtube video/playlist downloader, with convert to mp3, etc...
* [OBS Studio](https://github.com/obsproject/obs-studio): Free best ever screen recorder.
* [K-Lite Codec](https://codecguide.com/download_kl.htm): Video player.
* [Microsoft To Do](https://todo.microsoft.com): To-do list pinned on Start Menu.
